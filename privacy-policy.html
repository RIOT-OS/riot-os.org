Privacy Policy

This notice describes how the RIOT community uses the personal data we collect from you when you use this website or other websites on the riot-os.org domain

As RIOT community, we host the following services covered by this privacy notice:

  • https://forum.riot-os.org, our forum.
  • https://pad.riot-os.org, a service to create real-time collaborative markdown notes.
  • https://tickets.riot-os.org / https://pretix.riot-os.org, a service for registration of the RIOT summit.
  • https://ci.riot-os.org, our continuous integration (CI) service to build and test the RIOT source code in our Git repository for various boards.
  • https://riot-os.org / https://www.riot-os.org, this website.

Account Information

The following data is collected when provided to us:

  • Personal information:
    • The forum collects
      • Your username, email address and optionally your full name.
      • The content of your posts, data about bookmarks, likes and links you follow in order to share that data with others, through the forum.
      • The content of private messages that you send through the forum. We make private messages available to senders and their recipients, and also to forum administrators.
    • The collaborative markdown note service collects
      • Your username, e-mail address, and user profiles for GitHub that you may connect with via your login.
      • Your profile picture loaded from GitHub via your login.
      • Pictures that you upload within documents to the local filesystem of the HedgeDoc server.
      • Relationships between your documents and your user account. This includes ownership, authorship, and revisions of all changes made during the creation of a note.
    • The RIOT summit registration collects
      • Your email address to send you the registration tickets.
      • The email address, full name, and company you provide for each attendee you register.
      • Whether these attendees attend the event on site or remotely.
      • Other information related to the event organization, including but not limited to: optionally provided dietary restrictions, T-shirt size, and attendance of sub-events.
      • The nationality you provide for the attendees you register.
    • The CI service collects no personal information on our side and stores any login information locally in your web browser as a cookie (see section on cookies below).
  • When you use our following services, no additional data is processed or stored:

    • https://riot-os.org
    • https://www.riot-os.org

Furthermore the following information will be processed automatically by the forum and the notes service:

  • Device user agent
  • IP address

This data is stored for as long as your account remains open.

The data collected on the forum is required for providing the service. It will only be used for this purpose. This includes managing your account, compile statistics on the forum and topic popularity. Furthermore, the collected information is used to defend the forum from abuse and technical attacks and diagnose and debug technical errors.

If you log in to these services using GitHub, your user agent and IP address may also be exposed to GitHub.

We do not intentionally collect or process sensitive personal information such as any of the special categories of personal data specified by the GDPR.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit https://allaboutcookies.org/.

How do we use cookies?

We use cookies to improve your experience on our website:

  • Keeping you signed in.
  • Remember if you accepted cookies.

What types of cookies do we use?

The websites mentioned above use:

  • Functionality - We use these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember your previously selected preferences. These could include what language you prefer and location you are in. Only first-party cookies are used.

Privacy policies of other websites

The forum contains links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website and websites within the riot-os.org domain mentioned above, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

We keep the privacy policy under regular review and place any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 19 August 2025.

How to contact us

If you have any questions about our privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us.
